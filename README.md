Originally started in 2017, San Andreas Online used to be a SAMP server that aimed to replicate the GTA Online experience on GTA San Andreas. It started out as a love letter to the whole GTA franchise, and aspired to have the Online feature-rich experience be accessible to the less fortunate who can't afford to buy GTA Online/a PC powerful enough to run it, and also for those who love San Andreas of course.

The development has been stopped sometime in 2019, and was only picked back up in 2025. The SAMP ecosystem has known a lot of evolutions since then, and a team of familiar faces and newcomers revolutionized a platform that was once THE place to be for all San Andreas gamers and had become a closed-source abandoned mess at that point. A new open-source San Andreas Multiplayer platform has risen since : [open.mp](https://open.mp/). For users, it is essentially the same thing, but more user-friendly, more compatible between versions, and more secure, but it is also a revolution for server owners, and San Andreas Online development was restarted in this favorable context. Everything was re-made from scratch, except for some systems that were simply converted properly for open.mp and its new technologies.

Today, our goal remains the same: provide players with a freeroam server that has RP-like realistic features, but none of the rules, to give the SAMP community the freedom and the Hollywood-style action-packed experience that is GTA in the first place. While it uses the San Andreas game & engine, it will go as far as it can to be its own GTA in many ways, from the lore to the atmosphere, with custom radios, a storyline, NPCs around the map and pop-culture references from the media that inspired the GTA series (and even Driver/GTA-like games).

Right now, most servers are roleplay only, and the few freeroam servers that are still running don't have the GTA spirit and identity to them, as they are either breaking the lore, being inconsistent, lazy or even plainly unmaintained.

We will not disappoint you. The server will only be made public when it is ready to have a complete enough first version, a kick-ass rocking trailer, screenshots, and a stimulating gameplay, but you can already subscribe to watch our progress and invest in what could become a success story.
